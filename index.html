About the Game

A New Kind of Matching Adventure

Match & Merge is more than just another puzzle. It’s a full-fledged 3D matching and merging journey where every object, merge, and level is part of a bigger story. Clear match boards, collect chests, and unlock mergeable items to rebuild the farm and solve a bizarre family mystery!

  • Great for puzzle lovers and merge fans

  • Set on a whimsical countryside farm

  • Filled with mini-games, characters & merge trees

How to Play

Match. Merge. Transform.

  • Tap and match 3D objects on the board
  • Clear full rows to earn mystery chests
  • Merge items from the chest to fulfill tasks
  • Progress through story chapters with every merge
  • Play daily quests, event maps, and seasonal puzzles

Storyline: Where’s Audrey’s Pa?

A Merge Game with a Plot

You’re Audrey — a determined daughter returning to a once-great family farm. But something is... off. Why is her father clucking like a chicken? What happened to the windmill? Why is the garden full of sock gnomes? The answers lie in your matches, merges, and decisions.
